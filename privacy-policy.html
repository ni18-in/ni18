Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NI18 ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website (the "Site") and interact with our services. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. The information we may collect includes:

  • Information you provide: When you submit our contact form, we collect the name, email address, subject, and message you choose to share so we can respond to your enquiry.
  • Information collected automatically: Like most websites, basic technical data such as your browser type, device, approximate region, and pages visited may be logged by our hosting and analytics providers to keep the Site secure and improve it.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: Cookies or local storage may be used to remember preferences and measure usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your messages, requests, and enquiries;
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Site and our services;
  • Monitor and protect the security and integrity of the Site;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

To provide and operate the Site, we rely on trusted third-party services that may process limited data on our behalf:

  • Formspree — processes contact-form submissions and delivers them to us.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the Site.
  • Google Fonts and jsDelivr — deliver fonts, icons, and front-end assets.

These providers maintain their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except: (a) with service providers who help us operate the Site; (b) when required by law or to protect our rights; or (c) with your consent.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@ni18.in or via our contact page.
